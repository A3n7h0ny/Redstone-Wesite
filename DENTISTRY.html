Comprehensive Dental Care

Expert oral health services for patients of all ages

Your Smile is Our Priority

The Dentistry Department at Redstone Health Center provides comprehensive, patient-centered care for all your oral health needs. Our team of experienced dentists and dental specialists creates a warm, welcoming environment where patients of all ages feel comfortable and cared for.

We believe that excellent dental care goes beyond treating problems - it's about prevention, education, and helping you achieve the healthy, beautiful smile you deserve. Our approach combines the latest dental technology with compassionate care to ensure your dental experience is positive and effective.

  • Restorative Dentistry including Fillings and Crowns
  • Cosmetic Dentistry for Smile Enhancement

  • Pediatric Dentistry for Children's Dental Health

Advanced Dental Technology

State-of-the-art equipment for precise diagnosis and comfortable treatment

Modern Dental Care

Our Dentistry Department is equipped with the latest technology to provide accurate diagnoses, effective treatments, and enhanced patient comfort.

  • Digital X-rays with 90% less radiation than traditional films
  • Intraoral cameras for detailed examination and patient education
  • CAD/CAM technology for same-day crowns and restorations
  • Laser dentistry for precise, minimally invasive procedures
  • 3D cone beam CT imaging for advanced diagnosis and treatment planning
  • Digital smile design software for cosmetic treatment previews
