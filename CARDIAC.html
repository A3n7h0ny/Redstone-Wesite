Your Heart Health is Our Priority

The Cardiology Department at Redstone Health Center is dedicated to providing comprehensive, patient-centered care for all aspects of heart health. Our team of board-certified cardiologists and cardiac specialists work together to diagnose, treat, and prevent cardiovascular diseases.

We understand that heart conditions can be concerning, which is why we prioritize clear communication, compassionate care, and personalized treatment plans tailored to each patient's unique needs and lifestyle.

Cardiology Services

Comprehensive cardiovascular care for all your heart health needs

Preventive Cardiology

Comprehensive heart disease risk assessment, lifestyle counseling, and preventive strategies to maintain optimal cardiovascular health.

Cardiac Diagnostics

Advanced testing including echocardiography, stress tests, Holter monitoring, and cardiac CT scans for accurate diagnosis.

Interventional Cardiology

Minimally invasive procedures including angioplasty, stenting, and cardiac catheterization to treat coronary artery disease.

Heart Failure Management

Comprehensive care for heart failure patients including medication management, device therapy, and advanced treatment options.

Electrophysiology

Specialized care for heart rhythm disorders including ablation procedures, pacemaker, and defibrillator implantation.

Cardiac Rehabilitation

Supervised exercise programs, education, and counseling to help patients recover and improve cardiovascular health after cardiac events.
